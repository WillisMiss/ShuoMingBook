How to Use the Product
  1. Paint the Base: Remove the plaster donut and paint freely with the included brush.
  2. Decorate Creatively: Layer colors or add sprinkle textures for a custom design.
  3. Dry & Display: Air-dry in a cool place. Finished pieces can be displayed or gifted.
Precautions
Important Safety Information
  1. Non-Edible: Plaster and paints are for crafting only. Avoid mouth/eye contact.
  2. Age 3+: Adult supervision required for children under 3 to prevent choking.
  3. Storage: Clean brushes after use. Store the plaster base dry and out of children's reach.
