How to Use the Product
  1. Adjust the harness to fit comfortably around the cat's body- neither too tight nor too loose.
  2. Place the cat's front legs through the openings and fasten the buckle on the back.
  3. Attach the leash to the harness ring and let the cat get used to wearing it indoors before going outside.
Precautions
  1. Keep out of reach of children-the leash may pose a strangulation hazard.
  2. Never tie the leash to a fixed object; always use under supervision.
  3. Regularly check buckles and rings to prevent breakage and possible escape.
  4. Do not pull the leash suddenly- it may injure or frighten the cat.
  5. Do not allow the cat to chew the harness or leash to avoid swallowing fragments.
