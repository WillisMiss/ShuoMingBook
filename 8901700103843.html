How to Use the Product
  1. Carefully unfold the kraft paper bags to avoid tearing.
  2. Use them for gifts, shopping, or storing lightweight items.
  3. Fold the top for closure or seal with a decorative sticker.
Precautions
  1. Keep out of reach of children - suffocation hazard if placed over the head.
  2. Do not use for heavy or sharp objects that may puncture the paper.
  3. Keep away from fire and heat sources - material is flammable.
  4. Keep away from small children - bag poses a suffocation risk.
  5. Store in a dry place to prevent moisture damage.
Cum să utilizați produsul
