How to Use the Product
  1. Use tweezers to pick up false eyelashes and apply the accompanying glue along the lash line.
  2. Wait until the glue becomes tacky (semi-dry state), then align and gently press onto the eyelid base, starting from the outer eye corner.
  3. Secure by pressing the inner corner and middle section for instant voluminous and curled lashes.
Precautions
Important Safety Information
  1. The glue is mild and low-irritation; sensitive eyes may test on a small area first. Discontinue use immediately if allergic reaction occurs.
  2. If glue gets into eyes, rinse immediately with clean water. Seek medical attention if discomfort persists.
  3. Children must use the product under adult supervision and assistance.
