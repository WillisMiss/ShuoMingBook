How to Use the Product
  1. Activating the Light: Hold the gray grips on both sides of the flashlight and squeeze them repeatedly. This generates electricity through an internal magnetic coil-cutting mechanism, powering the dual front LED bulbs.
  2. Adjusting Brightness: The faster you squeeze, the brighter the light.The light will gradually dim when you stop squeezing; continuous squeezing is recommended to maintain illumination.
  3. Carrying and Storage: The flashlight has a lanyard hole at the end, allowing you to attach a lanyard for easy carrying or hanging on a backpack, keychain, etc.
  4. Application Scenarios: Suitable for power outage emergencies, nighttime hiking,camping lighting,children's toys,outdoor adventures,and other environments without a power source.
Precautions
  1. This product generates power through manual squeezing and has no built-in battery. Continuous squeezing is required to maintain illumination. It is not suitable for long-term, continuous lighting needs.
  2. Avoid using in damp conditions, rain, or water to prevent damage to the power generation mechanism or short circuits.
  3. Children should use the product under adult supervision, avoiding excessive force that could cause hand discomfort or damage the housing.
  4. If not used for an extended period, store it in a dry, well-ventilated place,avoiding direct sunlight or high-temperature environments.
  5. This product does not support charging or battery replacement. If it fails to light up or the brightness is abnormal, the internal coil or LED bulbs may be damaged;replacement is recommended.
Cum să utilizați produsul
  1. Pornirea iluminarii: Tineti manerul gri de pe ambele parti ale lanternei si apasatti repetat. Prin taierea liniilor de camp magnetic in interior, se genereaza current electric care aprinde cele doua becuri LED din fata.
  2. Reglarea luminositatii: Cu cat apasatti mai repede, cu atat luminositatea este mai mare. Dupa ce incetati sa apasatti, lumina treptat. Se recomanda sa apaasti continuu pentru a mentine iluminarea.
  3. Transport si depositare: Partea din spate a lanternei are un orificiu pentru snur,care poate fi folosit pentru a atasa un snur, facilitand transportul sau agatarea de rucsac, breloc etc.
  4. Scenarii de utilizare: Potrivita pentru situatii de urgenta in caz de pana de current,drumetii nocturne,iluminat in camping,jucarii pentru copii, expeditii in aer liber si alte medii far sursa de alimentare.
