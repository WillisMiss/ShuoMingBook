How to Use the Candle
  1. Lighting the Candle: During initial use, allow the candle to burn continuously for 1-2 hours until the top wax layer fully melts into a "memory ring," preventing "tunneling" (wax residue on the container wall).
  2. Daily Use: For daily use, limit burning to 2-4 hours. For extended burning, extinguish every 4 hours, trim the wick, and relight. Avoid drafts to ensure even burning.
  3. Wick Maintenance: Regularly trim the wick to about 8mm (too long a wick may cause high flames and fast burning; too short may suffocate the flame). This reduces soot, extends burn time, and ensures more even and stable fragrance release.
  4. Extinguishing: Do not blow out the flame! Airflow can carry incompletely burned wax particles, producing soot and compromising the fragrance. Use a wick dipper to submerge the wick in wax for quick extinguishing, or cover with the lid to suffocate the flame naturally.
Precautions
Important Safety Information
  1. After lighting, place the candle out of reach of children. Children under 6 years old must use it only under full adult supervision to prevent burns or accidental tipping.
  2. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight or high temperatures (to prevent wax deformation and fragrance loss). Tightly close the lid when not in use to reduce dust entry.
  3. Pregnant individuals, those with sensitive skin or fragrance allergies should consult a professional before use.
