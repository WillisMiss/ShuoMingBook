How to Use the Product
  1. Wearing Method: Separate the bracelet at the magnetic clasp and wear it on both wrists; it can also be worn separately, symbolizing independence and harmony.
  2. Size Adjustment: The elastic cord ensures a natural fit for any wrist size, requiring no additional adjustment.
  3. Storage Suggestion: When not wearing, it is recommended to close the magnetic clasp to prevent scratching the beads. Store the bracelet in a velvet pouch or jewelry box.
Precautions
  1. Avoid Strong Magnetic Interference: The magnetic clasp contains magnets. Keep it away from credit cards, phones, smartwatches, and other electronic devices while wearing to avoid affecting their normal function.
  2. Water and Moisture Resistance: The beads are made of natural stone or stone-like materials. Avoid prolonged contact with water, perfumes, cosmetics, etc., to prevent fading or material damage.
  3. Avoid Heavy Pressure and Impact: Although the beads have a certain hardness, strong impact may cause cracking or detachment. Avoid strenuous activities or compression by heavy objects while wearing the bracelet.
  4. Keep Away from Children: The magnetic clasp is a small part and poses a swallowing risk. Keep the product out of reach of children.
  5. Regular Cleaning: Gently wipe the bead surface with a soft cloth to maintain luster. If stained, wipe gently with a slightly damp soft cloth and dry immediately. Do not soak.
