How to Use the Product
  1. Wearing Method: It is recommended to wear it on the left wrist, as traditional crystal culture considers the left hand the "receiving" side, which helps absorb energy. If worn purely as an ornament, it can be worn on either wrist.
  2. Size Adjustment: Adjust the size using the sliding knots at both ends of the black braided cord to fit different wrist sizes. After putting it on, ensure the knots are secure to prevent slipping.
  3. Energy Purification: It is recommended to perform a monthly "cleansing" to restore the energy field. You can choose exposure to moonlight, purification on a crystal cluster, or gentle wiping with clean water. Avoid chemical cleaners to maintain its pure energy.
Precautions
  1. Avoid High Temperatures and Direct Sunlight: High temperatures may cause color fading or energy disruption. Avoid wearing it in saunas or during high-temperature cooking.
  2. Keep Away from Chemicals: Remove it before contact with perfumes, cosmetics, detergents, or other acidic/alkaline substances to prevent surface corrosion or loss of luster.
  3. Prevent Physical Damage: It is recommended to remove it during strenuous exercise, fitness activities, or household chores to avoid impact or friction with hard objects that could cause cracks.
  4. Avoid Forceful Pulling: Handle it gently when wearing or adjusting, avoiding forceful pulling of the braided cord or beads to prevent the knots from loosening or the beads from breaking.
  5. Child Safety Warning: This product contains small beads and metal accessories and is not suitable for wearing or play by children under 14 years old. Keep it out of reach of children to avoid the risk of swallowing.
  6. Storage Method: When not wearing it, wrap it in a soft cloth or place it in a velvet pouch and store it separately to avoid scratching from friction with other jewelry.
