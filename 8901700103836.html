How to Use the Product
  1. Press the metal clasp to open the box, and lay the lid flat to prevent damage to the hinge.
  2. Place necklaces, rings and other jewelry separately into the sponge grooves or velvet compartments to secure them.
  3. Close the lid firmly and lay the box flat among clothes in your bag to avoid crushing.
Precautions
  1. Avoid mixing with sharp objects; do not let the dark-colored box touch damp light-colored clothing for long periods.
  2. Do not wash with water or expose to perfumes or chemicals; if wet, wipe dry immediately with a cloth and air-dry in shade.
  3. Avoid direct sunlight and heavy pressure to prevent fading, deformation, or clasp failure.
Cum s utilizati produsul
  1. Apasati clema metalica pentru a deschide cutia si asezati capacul plat pentru a preveni deteriorarea balamalei.
  2. Puneti colierele, inelele si alte bijuterii separat in canelurile din burete sau compartimentele din velur pentru a le fixa.
  3. Inchideti capacul bine si asezati cutia plat intre hainele din geanta pentru a evita strivirea.
Precautii
  1. Evitati amestecul cu obiecte ascutite; nu lasati cutia de culoare inchisa in contact prelungit cu haine umede de culoare descrisa.
