How to Use the Product

This set includes 13 makeup brushes and a storage pouch. The functions of each brush are as follows:

  • Foundation Brush: Apply liquid or cream foundation evenly across the face.
  • Powder Brush: Use for setting makeup with loose or pressed powder.
  • Contour Brush: Sculpt facial features and create definition.
  • Blush Brush: Apply blush to the apples of the cheeks for a natural flush.
  • Eyeshadow Brushes (Various): Apply and blend eyeshadows on lids and crease.
  • Concealer Brush: Cover imperfections and dark circles with precision.
  • Eyeliner Brush: Create precise lines along the lash line.
  • Lip Brush: Apply lipstick evenly for a defined look.
  • Highlighter Brush: Add glow to cheekbones, brow bones, and cupid's bow.
    • Precautions
    Important Safety Information

    1. Clean brushes regularly with mild shampoo or brush cleaner.

    2. Store in a dry place away from direct sunlight.

    3. Do not share brushes to prevent cross-contamination.

    4. Replace brushes if bristles become damaged or shed excessively.

    5. Keep away from children under 3 years due to small parts.
