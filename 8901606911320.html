Product Usage Method

  • Inflate the balloons using the included hand pump to the indicated sizes.
  • Do not overinflate large balloons (18 inches, 12 inches, etc.) - inflate to about 80% capacity, while small 5-inch balloons can be fully inflated until they feel slightly firm to the touch.
  • According to personal preference, you can fix the balloon arrangement with ribbons or glue dots, or lay the balloons flat on the ground.
  • After fixing the balloon arrangement, you can attach metal butterfly decorations to the balloon surface using glue dots.

Precautions

  • Do not overinflate balloons. Avoid direct sunlight or proximity to heat sources (such as radiators, hair dryers on hot settings) to prevent explosion.
  • Do not store unused inflated balloons for long periods. It is recommended to deflate and store flat to extend their life.
  • The kit contains metal butterfly decorations - be careful as metal parts may puncture the balloon.
  • Adult supervision is recommended when used by children. The edges of the metal butterflies are smooth, but do not let young children chew on them.
