How to Use the Product

  1. This product requires no heating and sets curls through physical wrapping. Section your hair, take a dry or slightly damp strand, and wrap it around the curler.
  2. Secure the end of the curler firmly using the included hair clips or fastening bands.Keep in place for several hours or overnight, depending on hair type and desired curl level.
  3. Unwind carefully and remove the curler. Gently loosen the curls with your fingers and apply styling products if needed.
