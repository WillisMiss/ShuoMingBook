How to Use the Product
  1. Slide the adjustable bead to fit the bracelet length to your wrist size.
  2. Wrap the bracelet around your wrist and secure the hook or clasp.
  3. To remove, gently unhook the clasp and slide the bracelet off your wrist.
Precautions
  1. Keep out of reach of children - small parts pose a choking hazard.
  2. Avoid contact with water, perfumes, or chemicals to prevent discoloration.
  3. Do not pull the bracelet forcefully to avoid damaging the chain or clasp.
  4. Remove the bracelet before showering, swimming, or sleeping.
  5. Clean with a soft dry cloth after use and store in a dry place.

  1. A nu se lăsa la îndemâna copiilor - piesele mici prezintă pericol de sufocare.
