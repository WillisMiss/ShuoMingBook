How to Use the Product
  1. Secure the phone: Pull open the U-clamp, place the phone into the clamp opening, then release the clamp to lock it in place, ensuring the phone does not slip.
  2. Adjust the legs: Bend the flexible octopus legs according to the scenario and secure them to the desired surface.
  3. Set the shooting angle: The holder head rotates 360° - adjust it to the desired angle.
  4. Connect the Bluetooth controller: Turn on the phone's Bluetooth, search and pair with the holder's Bluetooth, then press the controller button to remotely take photos or record videos.
Precautions
Important Safety Information
  1. Bend the stand legs gently - avoid excessive force to prevent cracking.
  2. Phone weight must not exceed the stand's load capacity (within 0.5 KG).
  3. Avoid direct heat and water exposure for the Bluetooth controller to extend its lifespan.
  4. When not in use, fold the legs gently and store in a dry place to prevent dust or moisture accumulation.
