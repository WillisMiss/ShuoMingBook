How to Use the Product
  1. Use the included manual pump to inflate balloons according to their labeled size. Do not overinflate large balloons (18-inch, 12-inch, etc.) - inflate to about 80% capacity, while small 5-inch balloons can be fully inflated until slightly firm to the touch.
  2. According to personal preference, you can use ribbons or glue dots to secure the balloon arrangement or lay the balloons flat on the ground.
  3. After securing the balloon arrangement, you can use glue dots to attach the metal butterfly decorations onto the balloon surfaces.
Precautions
  1. Do not overinflate balloons. Avoid direct sunlight or proximity to heat sources (e.g., radiators, hair dryer hot air setting) to prevent bursting.
  2. Do not store unused balloons inflated for extended periods. It's recommended to deflate and store them flat to extend their lifespan.
  3. The kit contains metal butterfly decorations - be cautious as metal parts may puncture balloons.
  4. Adult supervision is recommended for children's use. The metal butterflies have smooth edges but do not let toddlers chew on them.
