Instructions for Use (English)

Purchase this product only from the official TruOrigin store to ensure authenticity and quality! Other stores cannot guarantee the product's authenticity and quality.

How to Use the Product

  1. Direct placement: Prepare the baking paper(non-stick baking paper) and lay it flat evenly in the air fryer basket. There is no need to grease with oil; ingredients can be placed directly on the paper surface.
  2. Applicable scenarios: It can be used for cooking French fries, chicken wings vegetables, etc. It is also suitable for baking egg tarts or pizza(requires a baking tray).
  3. Direct disposal after completion: After cooking, discard the paper along with the grease and residue. The air fryer only requires simple wiping.

Precautions

  1. Do not operate empty: During preheating or cooking, please place food on the paper. Heating the empty fryer may cause the paper to ignite.
  2. Avoid too little food: The food must cover the surface of the paper(e.g., a single chicken wing should be combined with other ingredients) to prevent the paper from being lifted by the hot air and coming into contact with the heating element.
  3. Size compatibility: Different brands of fryers have different inner diameters.Please measure the inner diameter before ordering and select the appropriate paper size.
  4. Storage conditions: Store the product in a cool, dry place to avoid damage from moisture. Keep away from open flames for safety.
