How to Use the Product
  1. Securely attach the selected blade to the handle before use.
  2. Use the knife only for cutting bakery products (bread, cakes).
  3. After use, disassemble the blade, rinse with water, and dry immediately.
Precautions
  1. DANGER: The blade is extremely sharp. Always handle with extreme care to avoid serious cuts.
  2. Never touch the blade directly with your hand; always use the blade guard or hold it by the handle.
  3. Keep out of reach of children - the blade can cause severe injury. Store the set in a safe place, inaccessible to children and pets.
  4. Do not attempt to catch a falling blade and do not use it for any purpose other than intended.
  5. Regularly check the blade for wear and replace it immediately if damaged.
