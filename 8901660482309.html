How to Use the Product

  1. Carefully insert the fixator's needles through the surface and lining of the duvet or duvet cover layers.
  2. Ensure the locking mechanism(the cap) is securely closed over the needle head to lock the duvet in place.
  3. To remove the fixator, press the lock's release mechanism and pull it carefully out of the material.

Precautions

  1. KEEP AWAY FROM CHILDREN AND PETS: This product contains sharp needles. Risk of puncture or swallowing. Always store away and out of reach of young children and pets. Do not leave components scattered.
  2. PUNCTURE HAZARD: These are sharp objects. Handle with extreme care to avoid accidental injury. Do not point tips towards the body.
  3. Check before use: Do not use if the lock is damaged, does not close fully, or if the needle is bent or blunt. Remove fixators before washing, ironing, or dry-cleaning the duvet. Do not sleep directly on the fixators.
